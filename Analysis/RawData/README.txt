Flux data from FBA is stored in these excel files. ONLY data relevant to paper is given here.
The last 3 columns represent the IDs of the carbon, nitrogen and CN pairs. For the all media data, only last column represents the ID.

###7 carbon and 7 nitrogen sources
FluxData49ReplicatesNoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants
FluxData49ReplicatesNoiseLevel3 - 5 C/N contaminants
FluxData49ReplicatesNoiseLevel5 - 10 C/N contaminants

###Flux data generated with maltose/7 nitrogen sources
FluxDataMaltoseNoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants
FluxDataMaltoseNoiseLevel2 - 20 C/N contaminants

###Flux data generated with Cytosine/7 carbon sources
FluxDataCytosineNoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants
FluxDataCytosineNoiseLevel2 - 20 C/N contaminants

###Artifical high uptake rate of Nitrogen
FluxDataCarbonNoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants
FluxDataCarbonNoiseLevel2 - 20 C/N contaminants

###Artifical high uptake rate of Carbon
FluxDataNitrogenNoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants
FluxDataNitrogenNoiseLevel2 - 20 C/N contaminants

###Flux data for 174 carbon and 78 nitrogen sources
FluxDataCarbonNitrogen10NoiseLevel1 - 1 C/N contaminants (>230MB, cannot put in github)
